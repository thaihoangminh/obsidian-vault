## **Slide 2 – Session Objectives**

**By the end of this session you will:**

1. **Recognise** why automation is critical for productivity and accuracy.
    
2. **Describe** what n8n is and understand its core concepts.
    
3. **Identify** key features and common use‑cases across departments.
    
4. **Apply** best practices for building and deploying maintainable workflows.
    
5. **Evaluate** next steps for launching a pilot after watching the live demo.
    

**Speaker Script**

> “Here is what I want you to take away today:  
> • First, a clear sense of _why_ automation matters—both in saved hours and reduced errors.  
> • Second, a mental model of what n8n is and how its building blocks fit together.  
> • Third, inspiration: concrete examples you can borrow for your own team.  
> • Fourth, practical tips so your first workflow doesn’t turn into technical debt.  
> • Finally, the confidence to propose a small pilot right after this meeting.”
